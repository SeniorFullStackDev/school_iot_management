SMART CAMPUS APP


The Smart Campus App will bring you a smarter education experience !

>Imagine being able to review all your classes in one click!

> Getting access to online libraries and secure database with all past projects!

> Ordering your lunch from the canteen menu with just your smartphone!

> Checking your Time table for daily updates!

> Seeing live statuses of Labs and Classes!

> Enhance environmental sustainability through resource management!

The implementation of a Smart Campus System with smart IoT devices will ensure a better energy and resource management by having a live monitoring of the statuses of the AC, lights, projectors, Pcs, network usage and so on in the different blocks of the campus. Sensors connect the campus online via the Internet, so that the collected data can be viewed and managed (admin access) in real time from a website.

Our Objectives


MISSION: Our mission for the Smart Campus initiative is bringing sustainable, eco-friendly and energy efficient development. Smart Campus, we mean a campus that is an efficient, safe, sustainable, responsive and enjoyable place to live and work, underpinned and enhanced by digital / internet based technologies.


VISION: Our vision is to help creating an enriching and rewarding experience by leveraging innovative technology to enhance the experience for both staff and students as they navigate the physical and virtual campus.
